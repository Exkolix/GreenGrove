Why Garden?

Gardening is a great way to boost your mental well-being and reduce stress while improving air quality. It promotes physical activity, supports biodiversity, and gives you access to fresh, organic produce right at home.

Commonly Planted Options

Discover the favorites that brighten every garden.

Aloe Vera

Aloe Vera is a flowering plant native to California and Mexico. It's a versatile and drought-tolerant plant that can thrive in various climates.

Tomatoes

Tomatoes are a popular vegetable grown for their taste, sweetness, and versatility. They're grown primarily for use in salads, stir-fries, and as a staple in casseroles.

Snake Plant

Snake Plant is a small, succulent plant native to the Pacific Northwest. It's known for its beautiful, spiny, and tender leaves that produce bright, yellow flowers.

lettuce

Lettuce is a popular vegetable grown for its taste, color, and versatility. It's grown primarily for use in salads, stir-fries, and as a staple in casseroles.
